Artist

Description

An artist is someone who creates visual art, including paintings, sculptures, drawings, and more, typically for self-expression or to communicate an idea to others. They work in various styles and mediums, ranging from traditional forms like oil painting to modern digital art.

Average Salary

The average salary of an artist can vary widely depending on location, medium, and experience. In the United States, the median annual wage for fine artists is around $50,000, but it can be higher for successful, well-known artists.

Employers

Artists can work independently, sell their art through galleries, or be employed by art institutions, museums, or as freelance contractors for businesses that need creative services, such as advertising companies, game studios, or publishing houses.

Related Careers

Related careers include graphic designer, illustrator, art teacher, and animator. These careers all involve creative work but in different forms, such as digital design or animation.
